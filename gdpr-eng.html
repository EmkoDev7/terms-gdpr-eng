PERSONAL DATA PROTECTION POLICY

Data Controller and Contact Information

This policy applies to the processing (use) of any personal data carried out by Reserviri.si (data controller) or on behalf of the data controller.

Data controller information:

  • Reserviri.si
  • Address: Address
  • ID: Company ID number
  • Phone: Contact number
  • Email address: Email address

Types of Personal Data We Process

  • Basic contact information (name, surname, phone number, email address)
  • Information about the use of our websites (clicks on links, time spent) and information about responses to our email messages (whether the message was opened, which links were clicked)
  • Data necessary for the fulfillment of contracts and service execution (selected services, price, color combinations, selected performer, payment information)

Legal Basis for Personal Data Processing

We may process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • When necessary to fulfill our legal obligations (e.g., issuing invoices for services provided, maintaining order records...)
  • When the processing of your personal data is necessary for the conclusion and fulfillment of a contract (service provision) that you have entered into with us or because you requested an offer from us
  • When you have given consent for the processing of your personal data for specific processing purposes, with the right to withdraw consent at any time (e.g., for sending promotional messages for services of other companies providing complementary services - taxi services, restaurants...)
  • When we have a legitimate interest in processing your personal data (e.g., sending SMS or email reminders to reduce the likelihood of absence).

Purposes of Personal Data Processing

We may use your personal data for one or more of the following purposes:

  • Ordering
  • Technical support in service provision
  • Communication with you regarding the provision of our services and responding to your inquiries
  • Contract conclusion and fulfillment of obligations from the concluded contract
  • Marketing communications (email, postal mail, SMS messages, and other electronic messages)
  • Marketing communication based on customized or personalized offers and messages, profiling, or grouping users, each of whom may receive marketing communications with different content. When creating profiles, we track individual activity (such as the amount of time spent on specific content, interests, and opening email messages) as well as the frequency and value of past orders
  • For the fulfillment of all legal requirements and dispute resolution
  • For statistical analysis of the sale of our services and the use of our online applications

How Long We Retain Your Personal Data and What Happens Afterwards

We retain basic personal data as long as you have the status of our registered user on our websites.

We permanently store personal data processed based on your consent or until you withdraw this consent.

We keep invoice data for 10 years from the issuance date.

We retain data necessary for the conclusion and fulfillment of contracts between you and us for an additional 5 years from the fulfillment of the contract (service provision).

After the retention period expires, we effectively delete or anonymize personal data, meaning that we process them in a way that they can no longer be linked to you or attributed to you.

Voluntary Provision of Data and Consequences of Non-Provision

Providing personal data is voluntary. You are not obligated to provide us with personal data, but if you do not, you may not receive certain services or enter into a contract with us. We will specify which data, if not provided, will have the mentioned consequences.

Who Has Access to Your Personal Data

We do not disclose your personal data, and we do not allow third parties outside the company Reserviri.si to access them, except for those with a written agreement with us, based on which they perform certain tasks related to data processing and are obliged to comply with laws related to the processing and protection of personal data (so-called Data Processors). Data Processors to whom we provide personal data are:

  • Marketing services provider
  • Email service provider
  • SMS messaging service provider
  • Business management software provider (ordering, invoicing, payment, business optimization...)

Data Processors are allowed to process personal data only within the scope of our instructions and must not process personal data for their own purposes. Along with their employees, they commit to protecting the confidentiality of your personal data. Data Processors do not transfer personal data to third countries (outside the European Economic Area; EU member states plus Iceland, Norway, and Liechtenstein), except to the USA - all Data Processors in the USA are enrolled in the Privacy Shield program.

Your Rights Regarding Personal Data, How to Withdraw Consent, and Consequences of Withdrawal

Regarding your personal data, you have the right to request from us at any time:

  • Confirmation of whether we process your personal data
  • Access to personal data and the following information:
  • Purposes of processing
  • Types of personal data
  • Users or categories of users to whom personal data have been or will be disclosed, especially users in third countries or international organizations
  • The envisaged period for which personal data will be stored or, if not possible, the criteria for determining that period
  • The existence of automated decision-making, including profiling, and the reasons for it, as well as its relevance to you and the foreseeable consequences of such processing for you
  • One (free) copy of personal data in a format you specify (if the request is made by electronic communication and you do not request otherwise, the copy will be provided in electronic form). We may charge a reasonable fee, taking into account the costs, for additional copies you request
  • Correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Restriction of processing when:
    • You dispute the accuracy of personal data, for a period allowing us to verify the accuracy of personal data
    • Processing is unlawful, and you oppose the deletion of personal data, instead requesting restriction of their use
    • We no longer need personal data for processing purposes, but you need them to confirm, enforce, or defend legal claims
  • Deletion of all personal data (right to be forgotten), if the conditions specified in Article 17 of the GDPR are met, especially if you withdraw your consent for the processing of personal data
  • Obtaining personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, with the right to transfer this data to another controller without hindrance from us
  • Stop using personal data for marketing purposes, including profiling
  • Not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing, including profiling, provided that the conditions specified in Article 22 of the GDPR are met

You also have the right to lodge a complaint against us with the Information Commissioner if you believe that the processing of your personal data violates the GDPR.

Procedure for Exercising Rights

You can submit your requests related to exercising your rights regarding personal data in writing to any of the contacts listed in the data controller. We may request additional information from you for reliable identification in case you exercise your rights regarding personal data, but we can only refuse to take action if we can demonstrate that we cannot reliably identify you.

We must respond to your request for the exercise of your rights regarding personal data without undue delay, and no later than one month from the receipt of your request.
